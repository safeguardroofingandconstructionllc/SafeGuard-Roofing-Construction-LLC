Knowledge Hub

Answering our clients' most common general contracting inquiries.

What types of roofing systems do you install?

We install all premium formats including asphalt architectural shingles, high-grade slate tile, metal seam roofs, and luxury modern flat roof membranes designed specifically for the local NY climate.

Are you fully insured and licensed as contractors in Flushing, NY?

Yes, absolutely. SafeGuard Roofing & Construction LLC is a fully licensed and comprehensive liability insured general contractor operating directly within New York State rules and municipal guidelines.

How long does a typical full roof replacement take?

Most residential custom roofing replacements are meticulously finalized within 1 to 3 business days, heavily depending on the structural scope, height, and chosen finishing design.
